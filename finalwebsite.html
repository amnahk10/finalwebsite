A Trip To Ashland, Oregon

  • Mount Ashland: a non-crowded, beautiful space to ski and snowboard, with a big lodge to rest in.

  • Lithia Park: a beautiful park right next to downtown, completely filled with beautiful trees, flowers, lakes, and animals.
